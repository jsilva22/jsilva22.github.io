Hello Guys and Welcome to my Website

This page was created for a course I'm in called Computing for the Web that is being taught by Professor Izbicki and it helped me get lots of practice with using HTML and CSS.

For this project, I brainstormed different possible topics and came up with lots of different ideas for my website. Ultimately though, I decided to do a tutorial for people just getting into Pokemon training. The three topics that will be covered are choosing a starter, evolving, and items that can be used during battle.

Before we get started, take a look at the following pokemon and see if any of these are familiar to you.









Each of these rows of pokemons represents a different generation of pokemon and a beginning trainer chooses 1 out of the given set of 3 pokemon!
